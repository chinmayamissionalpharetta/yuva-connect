Who are we?

JCHYK is the youth group wing of Chinmaya Mission Alpharetta. We are high schoolers, in grades from 9th to 12th, who are passionate about making a difference in our community. We do things such as volunteering and host events that can help everyone, from any grade, be successful in life.
